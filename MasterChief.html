The Master Chief

John-117

Master Chief, also known as John-117, is the iconic protagonist of the Halo video game series developed by Bungie and later continued by 343 Industries. Clad in the formidable MJOLNIR powered armor, Master Chief is a supersoldier and the last of the SPARTAN-II program. His face remains obscured by his distinctive green helmet, contributing to the enigma surrounding his character. A silent and stoic warrior, Master Chief is known for his exceptional combat skills, leadership abilities, and unwavering dedication to humanity's survival in the face of relentless alien threats, notably the Covenant and the parasitic Flood. With his trusty AI companion Cortana, Master Chief embarks on epic journeys across the vast expanses of the Halo universe.

The Armours


  • Mark I MJOLNIR Armor: The original armor, designed for the SPARTAN-I program. Limited shielding and lacked the advanced features of later iterations. First used during the SPARTAN-I training missions.

  • Mark II MJOLNIR Armor: Improved version of the Mark I, introduced during the early years of the SPARTAN-II program. Enhanced strength and shielding capabilities. Used during the early missions of the Human-Covenant War.

  • Mark III MJOLNIR Armor: The upgraded version of the Mark II, featuring refinements and improvements. Deployed during the later stages of the Human-Covenant War.

  • Mark IV MJOLNIR Armor: A significant advancement, featuring energy shielding and improved armor plating. First deployed during the events of the Fall of Reach.

  • Mark V MJOLNIR Armor: Enhanced shielding and improved energy efficiency compared to the Mark IV. Featured in the events of the original "Halo: Combat Evolved" game.

  • Mark VI MJOLNIR Armor: Notable for its role in the events of the second and third installments of the Halo series. Improved energy shielding, reactive circuits, and enhanced compatibility with Cortana.
